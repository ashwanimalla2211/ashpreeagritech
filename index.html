How agrivoltaics works

Panels are elevated and spaced to let light and rain reach the pasture. Animals graze beneath and between rows; we rotate paddocks to protect forage and soils.

1) Elevated racking

Rows are set for farm equipment access and animal movement. The array doubles as shade and windbreak.

2) Managed grazing

Portable fencing and water points support rotation, encouraging resilient grasses and clover.
